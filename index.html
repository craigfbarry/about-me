My Story

Once upon a time I was an electronic engineering student. After working for a short time in the industry I packed my bags to go and travel the world. I backpacked around Europe with the odd trip to Asia. Upon my return I embarked on a career in Information Technology. Over the past 10 years I have provided IT support in the Energy industry, Banking and Finance, the Mining industry and for Government. I have also begun studies in Web Development and look forward to the next chapter of my journey.
